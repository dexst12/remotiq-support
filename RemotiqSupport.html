Frequently asked questions
Why is my TV not being detected?
The most common cause is that your phone and TV are on different networks. Make sure both are connected to the same Wi-Fi network (not a guest network). Also ensure your TV's network connection is active and that the TV is powered on. If the issue persists, try restarting both your router and the app.
Does Remotiq work without Wi-Fi?
Remotiq uses Wi-Fi to communicate with your TV. For older TVs without built-in Wi-Fi, Remotiq may work via your phone's infrared (IR) blaster if your device has one. Most modern smart TVs require a shared Wi-Fi connection.
Can I control multiple TVs with one account?
Yes. Remotiq supports multiple TVs from a single app. Simply switch between devices from the device selector screen. All TVs on your Wi-Fi network will appear automatically.
The app connected but buttons are not responding. What do I do?
Try disconnecting and reconnecting to the TV within the app. If that does not help, restart your TV and reopen Remotiq. In some cases, a TV firmware update can cause temporary compatibility issues. If the problem continues, please contact us at dexst12@gmail.com.
How do I type text on my TV using the app?
Tap the keyboard icon in the remote interface. Your phone's full keyboard will appear, letting you type passwords, search queries, or anything else directly. This is much faster than using the on-screen TV keyboard.
Is Remotiq free to use?
Remotiq is free to download. Core remote control features are available at no cost. Some advanced features may be available through an optional upgrade. You will always be informed before any purchase is made.
How do I request a refund?
All purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com (iOS) or Google Play's order history (Android). You can also reach us at dexst12@gmail.com and we will assist you.
My TV brand is not listed. Will Remotiq still work?
Remotiq is compatible with thousands of TV models beyond those listed. If your TV runs Android TV, Google TV, Roku OS, or Fire TV, it is very likely supported. Contact us with your TV brand and model and we will confirm compatibility.

Privacy policy

Your privacy matters

Remotiq is designed to work locally on your home network. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from your TV activity, viewing habits, or remote usage.

The app requires access to your local network solely to detect and communicate with your TV. No data is transmitted to external servers for this purpose.

If you choose to contact support, any information you provide (such as your name or email) is used only to respond to your inquiry and is never shared with third parties.

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools that collect personally identifiable information.

If you have any questions about how your data is handled, please contact us at dexst12@gmail.com.
